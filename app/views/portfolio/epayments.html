Overview

A transformed card payment service for repayment of benefit debt. The service will begin to shift transactions out of the telephony channel, extend use of Behavioural Insight and build an early stage component of a future debt model service.

Project details

Location: Leeds
Contact: Bryan Sharp
Priority: High
Phase: Beta
Phase start date: late May 2015
Private Beta start date: October 2015
Estimated 'go live' date: January 2016
RPA rating:

Customer impact

  • Two million customers have benefit debt.
  • 24 hour access for customers to self-serve and business efficiencies and savings due to reduced telephone traffic.
  • Initial service only available for those able and willing to make a full repayment, which accounts for approximately one-third of customers.
