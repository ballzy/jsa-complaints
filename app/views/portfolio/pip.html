Overview

The Personal Independence Payment (PIP) digital service will allow claimants to apply for PIP online.

Project details

Location: Leeds
Contact: Steve Bruce
Priority: Medium
Phase: Beta
Phase start date: May 2015
Private Beta start date: 21st September 2015
Public Beta start date April 2016
Estimated 'go live' date: Summer 2016
RPA rating:

Customer impact

  • Will lead to business efficiencies and substantial savings by reducing the number of paper forms sent out.
  • It is estimated that 291,000 customers will use the digital claim between June 2016 and June 2017.
  • Reduced numbers of invalid claims by helping users decide if PIP is the right benefit for them.
