Overview

The project will optimise the use of HMRC’s earnings, employment and pensions data collected from employers on a ‘real time’ basis. The service will provide a User Interface, made available on internal staff desktops, and an alert service to notify benefit processing staff when a claimant’s employment and/or pension status has changed. Will subsequently improve the accuracy of benefit payments and reduce losses from fraud and error.

Customer impact

  • Initial application to Pension Credit. Plans to extend to other benefit services, starting with Housing Benefit in spring 2016.
  • New claims will be automatically subject to RTI data, enabling Benefit Agent to immediately assess the validity and accuracy of claim.
  • An Alert System will be applied to those already receiving benefits, enabling Benefit Agent to adjust benefit payments dependent on real-time earnings.
