Overview

The Secure Communications project is an enabling service that will allow third parties to be authenticated and then securely submit web based products digitally. The solution will allow us to deliver the ability to authenticate across a range of third parties and transactions in DWP and across government. The proof of concept for Alpha is focussing on authenticating GPs through their NHS smartcard system.

Customer impact

  • Potential business efficiencies from reduced processing times and no postage delays or costs. More secure than postal method.
  • Reduce time taken to process benefit claims and will act as a time saver for third party uses such as GPs.
  • Initial application to GP authentication in sending DS1500 form, which is used by approximately 20,000 people. Potential for much wider application, including on ESA, MAT B1, E prescriptions etc.
